Awesome Features

Personalized Daily Schedule

The angels create a customized daily schedule for each child, incorporating their school activities, extracurriculars, meal times, study periods, playtime, and relaxation.

Virtual Study Support

Angels provide virtual study support by helping with homework, answering questions, and explaining concepts. They can offer guidance, resources, and personalized study tips.

Health Monitoring

We enable the angels to monitor the child's health by tracking their physical activity, sleep patterns, and dietary habits. They can provide suggestions for healthy meals, exercise routines.

Progress Tracking and Reports

We also offer progress tracking features that allow parents and children to monitor their achievements and milestones. Generate reports detailing academic progress, skill development, and overall growth.

Skill Development Courses

We provide a range of skill development courses tailored to the child's interests and age group. These can include coding, robotics, music, art, creative writing, or any other area they wish to explore.

Parent-Child Communication

HappyAngels tries to enable seamless communication between parents, children, and the angels through messaging, video calls, or voice chats. This allows for regular updates, discussions, and sharing of concerns.
